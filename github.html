IS PERCEPTION MATTERS?

                          Many of us would be thinking that we need to be 100% in all. isn't it? Many of us would be in fond of getting the best in this world. Isn't it? Then, just spend few minutes to think what is bad in this world. The genuine answer is "NO". Because, you would be thinking that something is the best which may be hate by others. AM I RIGHT?

                          We of course the human, having same no of eyes but with different vision. So when two people do analysis on the same thing and if they were asked to conclude them, the both conclusion could never be same all the time.
Lemme take an example, if I and my friend are asked to select a mobile brand to buy, I will need some special features which may or may not be same by her. I may ignore the quality of camera and will stick on to the memory storage and some other features since my usage of mobile could be differ. At the same time she might consider the camera quality and may ignore the storage and battery conditions. Obviously we both will select different mobile brands. For me, my selection would be better than her's. For her, her choice would be better.

Conclusion: It doesn't mean that the mobile brand that was chosen by her is not good since I didn't choose.

                          Everything depends on our own perspective. The is no common best thing for all and at the same time, there is no bad thing for all. Everything differs. so, its better to analyse yourself in order to find what is good and the best for you than thinking deeply with other's words. I didn't mean that we should not consider other's words but, we should not stick on to them alone.
Think wisely and proceed further.
